Black Speech

This language is reffered to as Black-speech in RMSR, but can also mean Black Speech of Sauron. For the purpose of this document I have decided to unify several sources of Black Speech, to form a language as complete as possible. Referring links might pass between different systems. Black-speech words an phrases are translated ad-hoc in order to get a better understanding of the gramatical building blocks in speech, names and titles.

Note: Very few tribes or clans of black-speech uses family names. Given names and titles are most common.
Note: Many of the dark races keep the female only for breading, for that reason, female names are unknown or uncommon.
